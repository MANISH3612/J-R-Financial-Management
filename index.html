Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much one should Invest?

    Insurance: 10-20%, Savings: 10-15%, Medical: 5-10% and Others: 50-60%.

  • What is Grace Period?

    LIC - The grace period for policies having a monthly mode of payment premium is 15 days from the due date. The grace period for policies where the premium payment mode is quarterly, half-yearly or even yearly is one month from the due date.

    STAR HEALTH - No Grace Period.

  • What is Nomination?

    A nominee is someone who will receive the sum assured if you pass away within the policy period. They are the beneficiaries of the policy, therefore, it is important to choose your nominee wisely. Have multiple Nominations to avoid legal formality and smoth claim.

  • What is Revival?

    We can define revival as “to bring back to life”. The revival of life insurance policy is required when the insured fails to pay the premium within the grace period and the coverage of the policy lapses.The renewal of the lapsed policy can be done anytime within 5 years from the date of unpaid premium.The renewal of the ULIP policy can be done anytime within 3 years.

  • What Services do you provide? Are there any Service Charges?

Door step service is provided for all services like Premium Payment, Updating PAN/Aadhar/NEFT/MObile/Email, Change of Nominee, Loans on Policy if required, Revival, etc. All Services to our clients are provided at FREE of Cost.
